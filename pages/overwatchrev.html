Overwatch

In a genre occupied predominantly by YouTube montage videos highlighting twitch shooting skills of pre-pubescents, Overwatch delivers a more considered, tactical affair. A game about assessing and reacting, never before have I made as many moment-to-moment decisions in a shooter: on positioning, map layout, abilities, character selection, enemy position, everything. Overwatch is a first-person shooter that oozes personality and charm, but beyond that surface layer lies a deep, tactical game where your most powerful weapon is your brain. If, like me, you’ve recently fallen out of love with online first-person shooters, play Overwatch long enough for it to deliver one of its many standout moments and you’ll be renewing your vows in no time.
